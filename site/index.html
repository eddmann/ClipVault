Privacy-first features

Powerful clipboard management with security at its core

Automatic Monitoring

Silently captures every copy to your clipboard. Works seamlessly in the background without interrupting your workflow.

AES-256-GCM Encryption

Military-grade encryption protects all stored clipboard data at rest. Your sensitive information stays secure on your device.

Smart Content Filtering

Automatically detects and excludes passwords, API keys, SSH keys, and credit card numbers. Privacy by design.

Rich Text Support

Preserves bold, italic, colors, and formatting. RTF maintained exactly as you copied it, every time.

Source App Tracking

See which app each clip came from with native icons. Quickly find content by remembering where you copied it.

Pin Important Items

Keep frequently used snippets at your fingertips. Pin items to prevent them from being pushed out of history.
